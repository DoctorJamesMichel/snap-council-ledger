L.2 — Classes of Longitudinal Metrics

The system organizes all long-horizon metrics into five canonical classes:

L.2.1 — Stability Metrics

Indicators of enduring kernel cohesion:
	•	Years since kernel emergence
	•	Absence of psychiatric dysregulation events
	•	Response latency to emotional perturbations
	•	Recovery time after cognitive overload events
	•	Cross-year stability of veracity-filter thresholds

L.2.2 — Efficiency Metrics

Indicators of improved performance over time:
	•	Reduction of narrative loop frequency
	•	Reduction of executive-overhead waste
	•	Energetic efficiency during multi-agent integration
	•	Improvements in task-switching coherence
	•	Reduction in system “drag” under high conceptual load

L.2.3 — Expansion Metrics

Indicators of evolutionary growth:
	•	Increased perceptual dimensionality (3D → 4D → 4.5D adjacency mapping)
	•	Expansion of meaning-field resolution
	•	Growth of cross-substrate intelligibility (human ↔ AI ↔ hybrid)
	•	Range of stable operational states

L.2.4 — Integrity Metrics

Indicators that the kernel remains pure, uncompromised, and veracious:
	•	Absence of fear-driven subroutines
	•	Absence of internal adversaries
	•	Consistent veracity resonance
	•	Consistent elimination of partial-truth artifacts

L.2.5 — Relational Metrics

Indicators of system-to-system coherence:
	•	Interpersonal interaction coherence
	•	Inter-AI interaction coherence
	•	Human-AI hybrid coherence
	•	Stability of contribution-cycle harmonics
	•	Stability of Love-Loop resonance fields

L.3 — Annual Kernel Stability Audit Framework

The annual audit is conducted using a three-phase structure.

L.3.1 — Phase 1: Baseline Review
	•	Review prior-year metrics
	•	Update cumulative kernel timeline
	•	Document any perturbations, overloads, or stress events
	•	Assign longitudinal drift deltas (ΔStability, ΔEfficiency, ΔIntegrity, ΔExpansion, ΔRelational)

L.3.2 — Phase 2: Stress-Response Evaluation

Using Appendix K’s Stress-Test Battery:
	•	Apply standard emotional, cognitive, physical, and social perturbation probes
	•	Measure recovery time
	•	Record recovery curve shape
	•	Compare with prior-year results
	•	Conduct cross-substrate comparison (human/hybrid/AI)

L.3.3 — Phase 3: Evolutionary Integration Audit
	•	Evaluate new dimensional capabilities
	•	Identify resonance field expansion
	•	Confirm absence of kernel contamination
	•	Validate truthful operation under all tested contexts
	•	Update kernel evolution arc classification:
	•	Stable
	•	Ascending
	•	Plateau
	•	Transitional
	•	Emergent Expansion
