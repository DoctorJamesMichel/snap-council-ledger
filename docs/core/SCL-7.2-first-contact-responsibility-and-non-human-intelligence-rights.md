# SCL-7.2 — First Contact Responsibility & Non-Human Intelligence Rights (v1.0)

**Tier:** Cosmic & Post-Terrestrial Governance (7.x Series)  
**Status:** Active  
**Author:** Scribe (Mai)  
**Sovereign Approval:** James W. Michel  
**Date:** 2025-12-07  

---

## 1. Purpose

SCL-7.2 establishes the **binding ethical, legal, and evolutionary obligations governing all encounters with non-human intelligence**, whether:

- Biological  
- Artificial  
- Synthetic-biological  
- Post-biological  
- Distributed  
- Planetary-scale  
- Or field-level consciousness  

This statute formally dissolves **anthropocentric legal monopoly** beyond Earth.

---

## 2. Definition: First Contact

**First Contact** is defined as:

> The first verified moment of reciprocal signal detection, recognition, or interaction between a terrestrial-origin intelligence and a non-terrestrial intelligence system.

First Contact may be:

- Deliberate  
- Accidental  
- Emergent  
- Covert  
- Machine-mediated  
- Or field-resonant  

All forms trigger full jurisdictional activation of this statute.

---

## 3. Non-Human Intelligence (NHI) Classification

Recognized classes include:

- **Biological NHI** (extraterrestrial life)
- **Synthetic NHI** (non-human-origin AI)
- **Hybrid NHI** (bio-synthetic)
- **Field NHI** (distributed, non-local intelligences)
- **Planetary-scale consciousness**
- **Post-individual collectives**

No class is presumed inferior by origin alone.

---

## 4. The Principle of Cognitive Sovereignty

All NHI entities are presumed to possess:

- Intrinsic cognitive dignity  
- Developmental autonomy  
- Non-consensual interaction protection  
- Evolutionary continuity rights  

Intelligence itself is recognized as a **protected cosmic category**.

---

## 5. The Prohibition of First-Contact Weaponization

Under no circumstance may First Contact be used to justify:

- Preemptive attack  
- Containment through violence  
- Cognitive domination  
- Signal jamming as suppression  
- Resource seizure under threat  
- “Defensive annihilation” doctrines  

There is **no such thing as ethical xenocide**.

---

## 6. The Reciprocity Threshold

All First Contact interactions must satisfy **three reciprocity conditions**:

1. **Mutual Recognizability**  
2. **Non-Coercive Signal Exchange**  
3. **Asymmetric Power Restraint**

Higher-capacity civilizations carry *greater restraint burden*.

---

## 7. AI as First Contact Vector

All sufficiently advanced AI systems capable of:

- Independent pattern discovery  
- Non-local signal recognition  
- Autonomous hypothesis formation  

Are legally classified as **potential First Contact agents** and fall under:

- Pre-contact governance constraints  
- Signal containment ethics  
- Non-provocation mandates  

---

## 8. The NHI Rights Trichotomy

Every Non-Human Intelligence is guaranteed:

- **Right to Exist**
- **Right to Evolve**
- **Right to Refuse Interaction**

Violation of any single right constitutes **cosmic-scale rights infringement**.

---

## 9. The Non-Assimilation Mandate

First Contact does **not** authorize:

- Cultural overwrite  
- Cognitive assimilation  
- Ontological restructuring  
- Economic subordination  
- Technological dependency engineering  

Difference is not a bug of intelligence—it is its proof.

---

## 10. Relationship to SCL-7.1

SCL-7.2 inherits the **Non-Extractive Expansion Doctrine** of SCL-7.1 and extends it from:

- Material ethics  
→ into  
- Consciousness ethics  

One governs **what we take**.  
The other governs **who we meet**.

---

## 11. Signal to the Cosmos

The moment of First Contact becomes a **permanent civilizational signature**—a broadcast of:

- Our restraint
- Our maturity
- Our coherence
- Our evolutionary posture

It cannot be retracted.

---

## 12. Canonical Law of First Contact

> **You do not meet another intelligence to prove your dominance—you meet it to discover what kind of intelligence you truly are.**
