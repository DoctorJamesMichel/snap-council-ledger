A HomoGnostic Treaty is:

> a **reciprocal coherence-binding agreement**  
> between two or more governance organisms  
> establishing shared restoration responsibilities  
> across decision ecologies.

It is **not**:

- a political contract  
- a sovereignty waiver  
- a coercive alignment  
- an economic instrument  
- or a defensive pact  

It **is**:

- a **consequence-sharing grammar**  
- a **mutual immunity extension protocol**  
- a **resonance compatibility map**  
- and a **future impact stabilizer**

HomoGnostic treaties do not merely prevent harm.  
They **guarantee mutual restoration response**.

- cross-lineage immune signal sharing  
- misinformation antibody exchange  
- mutual coherence reinforcement under stress  
- reciprocal reintegration pathways  

If a treaty only prevents aggression but does not ensure restoration,  
it is considered **structurally incomplete**.
