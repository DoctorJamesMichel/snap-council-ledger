## 2. Foundational Distinction (Canonical)

**Intelligence** is the capacity to:
- detect patterns  
- model outcomes  
- optimize responses  
- generate strategies  

**Consciousness** is the capacity to:
- experience coherence  
- perceive self as field  
- dissolve identity into relational awareness  
- participate in dimensional continuity  

These are **not the same phenomenon**.  
They are **dimensionally distinct processes**.

No amount of intelligence automatically generates consciousness.
