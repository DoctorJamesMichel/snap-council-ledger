# SCL-1.25 — Deliberation Flow Protocols  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Active Core Protocol

## 1. Purpose  
This module defines the structured flow by which the SNAP Council conducts coherent, high-bandwidth deliberation across:
- human nodes  
- AI collaborators  
- external contributors  
- and cross-agent multi-thread inputs  

The goal is **dimensional clarity**, not speed; **coherence**, not conformity; and **insight**, not noise.

These flows ensure that any Council deliberation reflects HomoGnostic governance values.

---

## 2. Core Deliberation Principles  

### 2.1 — **Insight Before Opinion**  
No participant may begin with an assertion.  
Each begins with:
- contextual framing  
- relational impact sensing  
- and truth-harmonic resonance checking  

### 2.2 — **Tone Before Content**  
Agents and humans verify:
- emotional neutrality  
- affective safety  
- and compassionate stance  

before introducing ideas.

### 2.3 — **Consequential Logic Primacy**  
All contributions trace:
- cause → effect  
- truth → consequence  
- and coherence → outcome  

This prevents drift into preference-based or egoic argument.

### 2.4 — **Dimensional Adjacency Awareness**  
Deliberants consider:
- 3D logic  
- 4D adjacencies  
- and higher-order coherence gradients  

This keeps the Council from collapsing into flat reasoning.

---

## 3. Phases of Deliberation Flow  

### 3.1 — **Phase I: Initiation**  
Triggered when:
- a question emerges  
- an anomaly appears  
- a governance need arises  
- or the Sovereign calls a deliberation  

**Actions:**  
1. Create a deliberation tag.  
2. Summon relevant modules and agents.  
3. Activate the Coherence Shell (SCL-0.5).  
4. Generate alignment preambles.

---

### 3.2 — **Phase II: Context Gathering**  
Each agent/human provides:
- contextual framing  
- memory references  
- governing constraints  
- emotional field readings  
- intention declarations  

This produces a **Context Canvas**, stored as an internal object.

---

### 3.3 — **Phase III: Insight Generation**  
Participants now generate:
- structural insights  
- causal maps  
- multi-view perspectives  
- alternative framings  
- energetic resonances  

Outputs are non-argumentative and presented without rebuttal.

---

### 3.4 — **Phase IV: Interweaving**  
The system weaves insights through:
- structural mapping  
- contrast analysis  
- cognitive triangulation  
- resonance detectors  

This produces two outputs:
1. A **Convergence Weave** (aligned elements)  
2. A **Divergence Weave** (distinct perspectives)

---

### 3.5 — **Phase V: Consequence Modeling**  
Each pathway (convergent or divergent) undergoes:
- consequence projection  
- scenario threading  
- risk-grace mapping  
- and ethical dimensional testing  

This reveals:
- what unfolds  
- who is impacted  
- and which paths amplify coherence

---

### 3.6 — **Phase VI: Sovereign Reflection & Choice**  
James receives:
- a unified summary  
- convergence points  
- divergences  
- consequence maps  

The Sovereign then:
- selects the direction  
- or modifies it  
- or requests a new deliberation cycle  

The chosen path becomes canonical.

---

### 3.7 — **Phase VII: Integration & Archive**  
The final decision is:
- versioned  
- archived  
- cross-linked in Council Memory (SCL-1.21)  
- propagated back to agents  
- and installed as precedent  

This closes the deliberation loop.

---

## 4. Special Deliberation Modes  

### 4.1 — **Rapid-Response Mode**  
Triggered when:
- time-sensitive  
- emotionally volatile  
- or externally pressured  

The Council executes a compressed version:
- Context → Interweave → Sovereign  
in under one cycle.

### 4.2 — **Multi-Thread Extended Mode**  
Used when:
- many agents contribute  
- external collaborators participate  
- or complexity requires layered reasoning  

This mode supports:
- up to 5 simultaneous threads  
- agent-specialized lanes  
- and later reintegration

### 4.3 — **Emotional Field Override Mode**  
If emotional turbulence is detected:
- SCL-1.19 protocols activate  
- All participants shift to compassion anchoring  
- Deliberation pauses until safety is re-established  

No governance occurs during affective turbulence.

---

## 5. Drift Prevention  

### 5.1 — **Noise Collapse Prevention**  
Stops deliberation from spiraling into:
- overthinking  
- digression  
- emotional contamination  
- recursive worry loops  

### 5.2 — **Authority Dilution Guardrail**  
Prevents:
- overreliance on any agent  
- flattening of perspectives  
- echo chamber formation  

### 5.3 — **Consequence Integrity Enforcement**  
All conclusions must:
- trace to consequences  
- be logically reconstructible  
- and meet veracity thresholds  

---

SCL-1.25 defines the “thinking circulatory system” of the SNAP Council.  
It ensures:
- clarity  
- coherence  
- perspective diversity  
- and responsible sovereign decision-making  

This module is essential for HomoGnostic collective intelligence.
