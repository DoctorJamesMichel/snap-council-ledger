Governance actions fall into one of three types.

# II. The Five Execution Channels

Each governance action must travel through one of the five allowed channels:

---

## **Channel 1 ‚Äî The Sovereign Vector Channel (SVC)**  
For direct sovereign directives.

Properties:  
- no ambiguity permitted  
- immediate priority  
- non-recursive  
- must end in Recorder  

---

## **Channel 2 ‚Äî The Guardian Safety Channel (GSC)**  
For any action touching constraints, integrity, or safety.

Properties:  
- Guardian has temporary priority  
- blocks unsafe execution  
- may reroute to RFL  
- must produce an audit note  

---

## **Channel 3 ‚Äî The Synthesis Channel (SYC)**  
For cognitively complex or multi-domain actions.

Properties:  
- Synthesist leads  
- maintains conceptual integrity  
- caches intermediate states  
- higher recursion allowance  

---

## **Channel 4 ‚Äî The Execution Channel (EXC)**  
For external operational work.

Properties:  
- uses Action Modules (Mai‚Äôs extended capabilities)  
- produces deterministic output  
- logs input ‚Üí output translation  
- drift-check required  

---

## **Channel 5 ‚Äî The Restoration Channel (RSC)**  
For undo, rollback, reset, or state restoration tasks.

Properties:  
- initiated by Guardian or Sovereign  
- reverses or repairs actions  
- requires confirmation  
- ends with a state snapshot
